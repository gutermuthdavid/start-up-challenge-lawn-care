Tips and Tricks

I have aeration listed as one of the bullet points on the main page. I would recommend hiring a lawn service company to provide this service. A professional aeration machine is quite expensive and it is used only once a year.
I would recommend to buy a pushable spreader. It is not too expensive and you can use that for seeding as well as fertilizing throughout the year. There are a few things that are very important with the Fall treatment of the lawn. First and foremost, make sure you buy pure starter fertilizer that has no weed preventer or weed killer added because those additional chemicals can affect new grass growth. The Mid-Atlantic region of the United States is a hard climate for any type of grass but the tall fescue seed mix seems to yield the best results.
IMPORTANT! New seeds need water, so water the lawn for two weeks, twice a day for best results. During this two week period mowing is not recommended so the new grass can grow uninterrupted.
